Stuffed Peppers

Description

Stuffed peppers with ground beef and rice are topped with a seasoned tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cold water
  • ½ cup uncooked long grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 6 medium green bell peppers
  • 16 ounces tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Steps for making stuffed-pepper

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring water and rice to a boil in a covered saucepan. Reduce heat to low and simmer for about 20 minutes until rice is tender.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, heat oil, add ground beef, and cook until evenly browned and crumbly (7 to 9 minutes).
  4. Hollow out bell peppers by removing and discarding the tops, seeds, and membranes. Slice the bottoms if needed to make them stand up straight.
  5. Arrange the peppers in a baking dish with the hollowed sides facing upward.
  6. Prepare the filling by mixing cooked beef, cooked rice, 1/2 of the tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Spoon equal amounts of filling into each hollowed pepper.
  7. Mix the remaining tomato sauce and Italian seasoning in a bowl, then pour it over the stuffed peppers.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven, basting with sauce every 15 minutes, until the peppers are tender (about 1 hour).
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
